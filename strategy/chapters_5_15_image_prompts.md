# Chapters 5-15 Image Generation Prompts

This document contains specialized prompts for generating textbook illustrations for Chapters 5 through 15, maintaining the **Modern Anime Style** (Nanobanana style).

## General Style Guidelines
- **Style**: Modern Seinen Anime style (clean line art, high-resolution textures).
- **Format**: 16:9 Aspect Ratio.
- **Labels**: Circular numbers (① to ⑩) placed next to objects for vocab scenes.
- **Atmosphere**: Professional, bright, and culturally authentic.

---
