# Chapter 2: Cómo soy - Nanobanana Image Prompts

This document contains the image generation prompts for Chapter 2 (A1 & A2) using the Nanobanana agent.

## Style Requirements

**Base Style:** Modern Anime Style (Makoto Shinkai, Violet Evergarden influences).
**Lighting:** Cinematic lighting, vibrant colors, slightly warm and inviting tone.
**Labels:**

- Vocabulary images MUST contain exactly 10 clearly visible, numbered circular labels (① to ⑩).
- Number circles must be distinct, high-contrast (e.g., white text on dark blue/black circle), and placed directly on or pointing to the respective objects/people.
- **NO TEXT** should be generated anywhere in the image other than the numbers. No English, Korean, or Spanish text.

5. **Handsome (guapo):** Number ⑤ pointing to a very handsome young man.
6. **Pretty/Beautiful (bonita):** Number ⑥ pointing to a very pretty young woman.
7. **Young (joven):** Number ⑦ pointing to a teenager or young adult.
8. **Old (viejo/mayor):** Number ⑧ pointing to an elderly person.
9. **Long hair (pelo largo):** Number ⑨ pointing to a character with very long hair.


## 3. Chapter 2 (A2) - Vocabulary: Personality

- **Filename to match:** `ch02_a2_vocab_personality_v10.png`
- **Context:** Personality traits and character adjectives.
- **Image Description:** A lively classroom or cafe scene where different characters are displaying distinct personality traits through their actions.

3. **Funny (divertido):** Number ③ pointing to someone telling a joke or doing something silly, making others laugh.
