# Chapter 3: Mi identidad - Nanobanana Image Prompts

This document contains the image generation prompts for Chapter 3 (A1 & A2) using the Nanobanana agent.

## Style Requirements

**Base Style:** Modern Anime Style (Makoto Shinkai, Violet Evergarden influences).
**Lighting:** Cinematic lighting, vibrant colors, slightly warm and inviting tone.
**Labels:**

- Vocabulary images MUST contain exactly 10 clearly visible, numbered circular labels (① to ⑩).
- Number circles must be distinct, high-contrast (e.g., white text on dark blue/black circle), and placed directly on or pointing to the respective objects/people.
- **NO TEXT** should be generated anywhere in the image other than the numbers. No English, Korean, or Spanish text.

## 1. Chapter 3 (A1) - Vocabulary: Identity & Documents

- **Filename to match:** `ch03_a1_vocab_identity_v10.png`
- **Context:** Personal identification, documents, and information.
- **Image Description:** An administrative desk or an open briefcase showing various ID documents and completely filled-out forms.

**Label Mapping (Numbers 1-10 on the image):**

1. **ID Card (el DNI / el carné):** Number ① pointing to a national ID card.
2. **Passport (el pasaporte):** Number ② pointing to a passport booklet.
3. **Driver's License (el carné de conducir):** Number ③ pointing to a driver's license card.
4. **Student ID (el carné de estudiante):** Number ④ pointing to a student ID card with a photo.
5. **Certificate (el certificado):** Number ⑤ pointing to a formal certificate or official paper with a seal.
6. **Signature (la firma):** Number ⑥ pointing to a handwritten signature on a document.
7. **Address (la dirección):** Number ⑦ pointing to a house icon or address section on a form.
8. **Date of Birth (la fecha de nacimiento):** Number ⑧ pointing to a calendar icon on a form.
9. **Nationality (la nacionalidad):** Number ⑨ pointing to a flag or globe icon on a document.
10. **Profession (la profesión):** Number ⑩ pointing to a briefcase or job-related icon on a form.

**Nanobanana Prompt:**

```text

Exactly 10 circular numbered labels (1 through 10) are placed on these elements: 1 on the ID card, 2 on the passport, 3 on the driver's license, 4 on the student ID, 5 on the certificate, 6 on the signature, 7 on the address icon, 8 on the birthdate icon, 9 on the nationality icon, and 10 on the profession icon.
