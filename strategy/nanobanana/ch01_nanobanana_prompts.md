# Chapter 1: Mi cuerpo - Nanobanana Image Prompts

This document contains the image generation prompts for Chapter 1 (A1 & A2) using the Nanobanana agent.

## Style Requirements

**Base Style:** Modern Anime Style (Makoto Shinkai, Violet Evergarden influences).
**Lighting:** Cinematic lighting, vibrant colors, slightly warm and inviting tone.
**Labels:**

- Vocabulary images MUST contain exactly 10 clearly visible, numbered circular labels (① to ⑩).
- Number circles must be distinct, high-contrast (e.g., white text on dark blue/black circle), and placed directly on or pointing to the respective objects/people.
- **NO TEXT** should be generated anywhere in the image other than the numbers. No English, Korean, or Spanish text.

## 3. Chapter 1 (A2) - Vocabulary: Health & Hospital

- **Filename to match:** `ch01_a2_vocab_hospital_v10.png`
- **Context:** Hospital, sickness, and medical terms.
- **Image Description:** A cross-section or wide view of a modern clinic or hospital room. There is a patient in bed, a doctor examining them, a nurse nearby, and various medical items.

**Label Mapping (Numbers 1-10 on the image):**

1. **Fever (la fiebre):** Number ① pointing to a thermometer in the patient's mouth or showing a high temperature reading.
2. **Cough (la tos):** Number ② pointing to the patient coughing (maybe holding a tissue).
3. **Pharmacy (la farmacia):** Number ③ pointing to a pharmacy sign or area visible through a window.
4. **Pill (la pastilla):** Number ④ pointing to a bottle of pills on the bedside table.
5. **Doctor (el médico):** Number ⑤ pointing to the doctor (wearing a white coat).
6. **Nurse (el enfermero):** Number ⑥ pointing to the nurse.
7. **Certificate (el certificado médico):** Number ⑦ pointing to a medical certificate/paperboard on a clipboard.
8. **Bed (la cama):** Number ⑧ pointing to the hospital bed.
9. **Hospital (el hospital):** Number ⑨ pointing to a hospital logo or cross on the wall.
10. **Patient (el paciente):** Number ⑩ pointing to the patient in the bed.

**Nanobanana Prompt:**

```text
A modern anime-style illustration of a bright hospital room. A patient is in bed, coughing slightly with a thermometer (fever). A doctor in a white coat is examining them, and a nurse is standing nearby holding a clipboard (medical certificate). Pills are on the bedside table. Through the window, a pharmacy sign is visible. A hospital cross logo is on the wall. Cinematic lighting, clean and professional atmosphere. There are exactly 10 circular numbered labels (1 through 10) placed clearly on these elements: 1 on a thermometer, 2 on the coughing patient, 3 on the pharmacy sign outside, 4 on pills, 5 on the doctor, 6 on the nurse, 7 on the clipboard, 8 on the bed, 9 on the hospital logo, and 10 on the patient.
```
