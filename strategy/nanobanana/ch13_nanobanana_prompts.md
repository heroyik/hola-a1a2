# Chapter 13 "Nanobanana" (v2.0) Image Prompts

These prompts are optimized for the **v2.0 Gold Standard** (Modern Anime Style/Nanobanana). Use these with high-quality generators (e.g., Gemini 3 Pro Image).

### Core Style Requirements

- **Style**: Makoto Shinkai/Violet Evergarden background art.
- **Lighting**: Transitions between morning light and evening glow.
- **Rules**: NO AR/digital overlay effects. NO text. Strictly circular white labels with black numbers.
